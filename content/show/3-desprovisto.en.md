- md: |
    Deprived of everything except ourselves, the eternal search to free ourselves from appearances, prejudices, and roles, showing ourselves naked and vulnerable to the world, where we will always find the other in moments of doubt or danger. DESproVISTO is a bet on plasticity, flexibility, and change, realizing that not everything has to always be the same way.
  imgs:
  - /u/espectaculo/desprovisto/62.jpg
- md: |
    **DESproVISTO** is the third work by the company UpArte, a work that maintains the essence of its previous works, which stand out for a high acrobatic technical level, performers without characters, and a leitmotiv that moves the work, but which is not a priority to know to enjoy the show. A search for balance between risk, rhythm, aesthetics, and humor. At the same time, DESproVISTO aims to make a change by moving from the rigid to the flexible, a base concept of the work that can be seen in the elements used on stage (fabrics) and in the bodily proposal, incorporating contemporary dance into the company's work.
  imgs:
  - /u/espectaculo/desprovisto/51.jpg
- md: |
    DESproVISTO is a contemporary circus work for all audiences, which aims to reach the viewer in depth and form, where the artists will experience a transformation process where acrobatic technique and dance will be part of their common language.
