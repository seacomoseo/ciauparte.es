- md: |
    **Todo encaja** is a fresh, visual work, where its six components will infect the viewer with the joy of their music and more intense emotions through banquine jumps, flights, and human towers, showing the most personal and fun side of acrobatics. A demonstration of overcoming and trust, full of enthusiasm and with a great capacity to spread it. The entire show has a frantic, fresh, and complicit rhythm, within a simple space that will make the audience enjoy the show.

    > <cite>Circada OFF Festival Jury (2014)</cite>
